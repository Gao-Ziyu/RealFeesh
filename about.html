About Us


Our Mission : To encourage everyone to be real, free, and to follow their passions

Gao Ziyu and Leong Yen Zhen, two friends, launched Real Feesh in 2023 with the goal of offering the highest-quality footwear while encouraging people to be real and escape becoming trapped in a job cycle and losing sight of their passions. We believe that wearing comfortable shoes will help one boost confidence and do well in life. Therefore, Reel Feesh is here!
